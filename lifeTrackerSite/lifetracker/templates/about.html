LifeTracker

LifeTracker is an application to help you better understand all the events in your life.

On average, how much time do you spend exercising per day? How much of your time is spent sleeping? How much of your time do you spend doing things that don't matter?

LifeTracker was specifically designed to help solve these problems.

College Student Mike

Mike is an average college student. He is cool with the guys, and generally gets good grades. But recently, his grades have been slipping a little bit. What happened?

Mike creates an account at LifeTracker, and after a few weeks of reccording events, he finds out what his problem was:

He spends 20% of his time hanging out with friends and 15% of his time at parties. Tisk tisk Mike.
